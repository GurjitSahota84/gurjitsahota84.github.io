Case Study: Lead Management System

Designed a tailored Lead Management System (LMS) for Automotive Avenue, a multi-location automotive dealership chain based in Colorado, USA. The platform was designed to simplify lead handling, inventory access, appointment booking, and cross-team collaboration for a better sales experience.

Role:

Lead UX/UI Designer, I collaborated on business requirements, Discovery & Research, Journey mapping for in-store and online lead flows, Personas built for sales agents and managers Wireframes & Prototypes, Card-based UI for lead visibility, location filters and map view for inventory, Appointment module with intuitive scheduling, Usability Testing, Real user testing with sales teams in Colorado, Iterations based on feedback (e.g., lead reassignment flow improvements), Design Delivery, High-fidelity Figma prototypes, Developer handoff with style guide and design tokens, Responsive design for tablets and desktops.

Project Blueprint

  • Centralize and automate lead capture and tracking
  • Provide location-based inventory search and filtering
  • Enable seamless test drive and service appointment scheduling
  • Support multi-role lead assignment (Sales, Finance, Service)
  • Deliver actionable lead performance metrics and reminders

The Challenge

Automotive Avenue faced several operational challenges:

  • Fragmented lead sources (web, phone, in-person)
  • Limited visibility into inventory across multiple Colorado branches
  • Manual appointment booking prone to conflicts
  • Delays in lead response due to lack of coordinated handoffs

The Approach & Solution

Inventory Listing by Location:

  • Smart filters: make, model, price, mileage, location
  • Location-aware inventory display with Google Maps integration
  • Live stock sync with internal systems across Colorado branches

Appointment Scheduling

  • Visual calendar for sales teams to manage availability
  • Customer-facing booking with real-time confirmations
  • Integrated reminders and follow-up notifications

Lead Dashboard

  • Kanban-style lead cards by stage
  • Quick actions: call, email, schedule
  • Performance analytics: response time, conversion rate, sales rep activity

The Results

  • 40% improvement in lead response time
  • 70% reduction in appointment overlaps
  • 35% boost in test drive-to-sale conversion rate
  • Improved coordination across sales, finance, and service departments
